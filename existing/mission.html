Mission

Vision and Mission:

The goal is to build and operate Sri Venkateswara (Balaji) Temple

  • With Lord Venkateswara (Balaji) as the *Main* deity
  • Per Agama and Vasthu Sasthras
  • Garba Gudi/Sannidhi (Sanctum Sanctorum) for Lord Venkateswara (Balaji)
  • Fulfill the spiritual and religious needs of the Hindus in CO
  • Promote Hindu music, dance, art, culture, yoga and meditation.
