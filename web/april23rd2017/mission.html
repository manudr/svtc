Vision and Mission
Build Sri Venkateswara (Balaji) Temple with the back drop of majestic rocky mountains in Castle Rock, Colorado as per Agama & Vasthu shastras. Provide Hindu religious services as per Vedic traditions. To fulfill the cultural and spiritual needs of devotees by promoting Classical music, dance, art, scriptures and meditation. Benefit the future generations with Hindu culture and traditions to be responsible and ethical citizens in promoting world peace and brotherhood.
